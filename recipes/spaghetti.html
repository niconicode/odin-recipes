Spaghetti

Description

Easy beef spaghetti recipe made with cottage cheese, cream cheese, and sour cream.

Cook and prep time: 1 hour

Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 package spaghetti
  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 16 ounce jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 / 2 cup butter, sliced
  • 8 ounces cottage cheese
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 / 4 cup sour cream
  • 8 ounces sharp cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Farenheit or 175 degrees Celsius.
  2. Bring large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti in boiling water until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 8 to 12 minutes. Drain.
  3. While pasta is cooking, heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutres. Drain and discard grease.
  4. Transfer beef to a bowl and mix in spaghetti sauce.
  5. Place half of the butter slices into the bottom of a 9x13 inch casserole dish. Spread half of the spaghetti into the dish.
  6. Mix cottage cheese, cream cheese, and sour cream together in a bowl. Spread mixture over spaghetti.
  7. Cover with remaining spaghetti and top with remaining slices of butter.
  8. Pour ground beef mixture over spaghetti and spread in even layer.
  9. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes. Spread cheddar cheese on top and continue baking until cheese has melted and is lightly browned, about 15 minutes more. Then Serve.
